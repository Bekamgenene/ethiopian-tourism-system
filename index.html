Popular Destinations

Lake Tana

Lake Tana is the largest lake in Ethiopia and the source of the Blue Nile. It is known for its beautiful monasteries and diverse wildlife.

Blue Nile Falls

The Blue Nile Falls, also known as Tis Issat, is one of Ethiopia's most spectacular natural attractions, with water cascading down from a height of 45 meters.

Lake Wenchi

Lake Wenchi is a crater lake located in the Ethiopian Highlands. It is known for its stunning scenery and opportunities for hiking and horseback riding.
